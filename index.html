FREEFIRE ONE TAP HEADSHOT BEST TRICKS


Garena Freefire Battlegounds is not a game of only killing, you have to kill enemies by headshots.
In Clash Squad mode, you take short range fight and here you can take the M1887[2 Ammo Shotgun] and you have to give your enemy a severe shock by killing him/her by a One tap headshot.
So, use these tricks.
  • Switch the gun
  • Make sure your aim is on the chest level of the enemy
  • Drag the fire button upwards
  • Follow these few steps and start it on your gameplay
    • Titbit: If enemy is going to right side, just drag the fire button up-right corner and same as left[up-left corner]

    HOW TO BECOME A PRO PLAYER IN FREEFIRE


    All Freefire players want to become a pro player in this game. So, these steps will help you.
    1. You have to play ranked matches
    2. Play solo vs squad
      • Select Squad mode
      • Disable auto-match right above the start button
    3. Land near a multi-window house
    4. Do house ruuning practise by using all of your custom HUD buttons to set your hands on your HUD
    5. Make sure when you are passing a window, you don't have to crouch or sit
    6. Use the Headshot Trick
    7. Use gloo walls
    8. Use cooked grenades
    9. Take one Short-range gun[M1014] and a Medium-range gun[UMP]
