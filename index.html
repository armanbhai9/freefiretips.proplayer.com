Tip set 1 (Ruins of helmets)


Headshot pro tips
Freefire is not a game where you have to show the number of kills. You should kill enemies wih headshots as most of the players know the headshot trick and can take you to your downfall. You must be ready for that!
See if these help you:
  • Keep the aim at the chest level
  • Drag the fire button upwards*
  • Practise in training grounds
  • *If the enemy is running at left side, drag the button to up-left corner and same as right (up-right corner)

    • Now let's see how to give a severe shock to the enemies by one tap headshots!
One tap headshots
Learnt headshots? Well, now are you wishing to learn one tap headshots? I am there to mitigrate your workload to some extent.Learn the one tap headshot trick.
  • Keep the aim at the chest level
  • Drag the fire button upwards
  • Practice in Training grounds
You are thinking that these are the same as the headshot trick, right? Hmm...There are some changes here that are points but not tips.
  1. One tap should be fired once
  2. Make sure the aim is at chest of the enemy
  3. The fire button should not be released suddenly after dragging, let the bullets go and then release the button

Tip set 2 (Grow up)


Clash Squad (CS) Advanced tips
There are some clash squad matches which are short range fights. Accept the challenge and fight against your opponent. Don't be afraid.
  • Don't use secret places as it will make you noob. Your skills will not ennoble itself if you play secretly.
  • Headshot is mandatory.
  • Use the gloo walls. Don't save it for future. Use where needed.
  • Always take cover.
